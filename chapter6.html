Bprs

Next longitudinal Bprs data will be analyzed. This data represents 40 male subjects that were receiving different treatment for eight weeks during which they were rated on brief psychiatric rating scale (BPRS). It is used to evaluate the level of schizophrenia.

This is defenately better model than the first one, as it doesnt allow slope randomness meaning that all the lines are parallel. Also, third model dont cahnge a thing compared to this second one. Always need to remember that this is just a model, and that exact fit is impossible to get.
